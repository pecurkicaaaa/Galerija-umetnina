About us

The Art Gallery was founded in June 2015 with the aim of encompassing masterpieces that have marked the history of art.

The gallery offers the opportunity to submit offers and purchase available artworks. Years of work and good collaboration have been key to our success and leaving behind thousands of satisfied customers. We take pride in our collection of awards that confirms our dedication to art. Our gallery has been recognized for its outstanding contribution to art promotion.

Currently, our gallery offers paintings, sculptures, and other artworks. In the future, it will be possible to purchase various products made of ceramics, clay, porcelain, and more on our website...




Awards we have won:

  • ○ Award for Artistic Selection - awarded by the Museum of Contemporary Art, 2018.

  • ○ Award of the Art Gallery "Nadežda Petrović", 2021.

  • ○ Best Museum Award - awarded by the Museum Society of Serbia, 2023.
